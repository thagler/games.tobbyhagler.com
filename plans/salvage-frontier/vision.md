# Vision

## Core Fantasy
You fight through hostile transit lanes, land briefly on dangerous worlds, harvest what you can, fortify a temporary outpost, and escape before being overrun.

## Experience Goals
- readable moment-to-moment play
- strategic decisions over twitch intensity
- strong risk/reward tension
- short, repeatable runs
- systems that feed each other cleanly

## What This Game Is Not
- not a high-APM pure bullet hell
- not a long-form base builder
- not a content-heavy RPG at prototype stage

## Target Feel
Thoughtful, coffee-friendly, strategic, replayable.
