# Prototype Scope

## Objective
Prove one complete playable loop before expanding systems.

## Included
- one ship
- bounded movement + auto-fire
- three enemy types
- salvage + fuel economy
- basic landing to outpost transition
- minimal structures (turret, wall, extractor/refinery)
- 2 to 4 outpost waves
- 3 route choices

## Excluded
- deep research systems
- multiple ship classes
- broad content packs
- full narrative layer
- polished art production
- broad progression balancing
