# Resources And Economy

## Prototype Currencies
- `salvage`: main gathered resource
- `fuel`: launch requirement

## Design Intent
- salvage drives build and preparation decisions
- fuel creates urgency and clear launch condition
- economy is simple but supports risk/reward tension

## Prototype Rules
- salvage gained mostly from transit combat and outpost extraction
- fuel earned via extractor/refinery or equivalent mechanic
- launch blocked until minimum fuel threshold is met

## Prototype Constants (Placeholder Defaults)
- `fuel_to_launch`: 100
- `turret_cost`: 30 salvage
- `extractor_cost`: 45 salvage
- `wall_cost`: 15 salvage
- `waves_per_outpost`: 3
