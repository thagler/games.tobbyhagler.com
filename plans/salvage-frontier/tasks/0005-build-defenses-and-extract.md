# 0005 Build Defenses And Extract

## Objective
Implement minimal defense waves and extraction/refuel mechanics.

## Constraints
- keep structure set to turret, wall, extractor/refinery
- cap outpost waves to 2 to 4

## Acceptance Criteria
- turret attacks enemies
- waves pressure outpost
- extractor/refinery produces salvage or fuel
- launch condition tied to fuel threshold

## Non-Goals
- broad tower roster
- deep economy balancing

## Test Steps
1. Build defense structures.
2. Run outpost waves.
3. Confirm extraction/refuel behavior and launch unlock.
